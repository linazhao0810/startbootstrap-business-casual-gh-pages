Kaweh Coffee Origin

The word "coffee" comes from the Greek word "Kaweh", meaning "strength and enthusiasm". Coffee trees are perennial evergreen shrubs or small trees of the rupee family. The coffee consumed daily is made from coffee beans with various cooking utensils, while coffee beans refer to nuts in the fruit of the coffee tree and baked by proper baking methods.
